bolt.diy is a platform for DIY enthusiasts to share and discover projects.

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage.

To upload a project, navigate to your profile and click the "Upload Project" button.

We support various file formats including .pdf, .doc, .txt, and .zip.

You can contact support by sending an email to support@bolt.diy.
